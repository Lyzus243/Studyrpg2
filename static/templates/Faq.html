StudyRPG FAQ & User Guide

Frequently Asked Questions

What is StudyRPG?

StudyRPG is a gamified learning platform where your study progress translates into XP, levels, skills, and rewards.

How do I earn XP?

You earn XP by completing quests, using study tools (Pomodoro, Flashcards, Memory training), and finishing daily challenges.

What happens when I level up?

Each level gives you access to new abilities, skill points, and sometimes unlocks new features.

Can I study with friends?

Yes! Join or create Study Groups to collaborate, share quests, and even take part in group battles.

What are Boss Battles?

Boss Battles are challenges that test your consistency and study performance. Winning grants XP, rewards, and achievements.

What if I forget to study one day?

Missing a day will reset your streak. However, you can regain momentum by starting new quests.

How do I use the Shop?

The Shop allows you to spend earned currency on power-ups, study aids, and cosmetic items.

What is the AI Assistant for?

The AI Assistant gives you personalized study tips, helps generate learning materials, and suggests areas to focus on.

Where can I track my progress?

Go to the Analytics section for detailed stats on your learning patterns and growth.

How do I reset my password or log out?

- To log out, click the logout button on the sidebar.
- For password reset, go to your Profile settings.

User Onboarding Guide

1. Sign Up or Log In

Create your account and upload an avatar if you'd like. Once logged in, you'll be welcomed on your dashboard.

2. Explore Your Dashboard

View your XP, Level, Streak, and Skill Points at the top. These stats grow as you complete study activities.

3. Complete the Interactive Tutorial

Go to "Getting Started" under the Overview section. Learn how to use all StudyRPG features step by step.

4. Start Your First Quest

Navigate to the Quests section. Choose a study mission to earn XP and rewards.

5. Use Study Tools

Try the Pomodoro Timer to boost focus. Train your memory with exercises. Create and review Flashcards. Ask the AI Study Assistant for personalized help.

6. Level Up & Unlock Skills

As you complete activities, you'll level up. Unlock and upgrade abilities in the Skills section.

7. Join Social & Battle Activities

Team up with Study Groups for collaborative learning. Challenge yourself in Boss Battles (solo or group).

8. Track Your Progress

Visit the Analytics section to view learning insights. Check your achievements and leaderboard rank.

9. Shop for Items

Use earned rewards to buy tools, power-ups, or cosmetics in the Shop.

10. Stay Motivated

Keep your streak alive by studying daily. Watch your profile grow as you progress.
