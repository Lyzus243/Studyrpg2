Welcome to StudyRPG

StudyRPG is not just a study tool — it's a full-blown gamified learning experience where your academic journey becomes an exciting, and addictive RPG adventure.

Customize your character by providing a unique avatar image URL, embark on learning quests, earn XP for completing tasks, and use XP Potions and Study Tokens to gain rewards. Unlock achievements, level up, and explore new abilities in a dynamic Skill Tree as you grow stronger in your studies.

Face off in epic Boss Battles — tackle tough topics through AI-generated quizzes. Win, gain loot, and document your victories in your adventure log.

Stay consistent and grow your Daily Streak. Track XP, Level, and Skill Points with real-time stats. Use AI-powered flashcards, practice tests, and concept explanations tailored to your needs.

Team up in Study Groups, showcase achievements, and shop rare gear and power-ups using earned coins. As your knowledge grows, so does your power!
